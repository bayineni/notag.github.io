Why Do We Tag People in Teams & Other Apps?

Tagging (using @mentions) is a helpful way to grab someone's attention in a busy chat or channel. It ensures the right people see important updates or actions that require their input.

Proper Uses of Tagging:

  • Highlighting key action items for someone in a group chat or channel.
  • Ensuring important updates reach the relevant team members.
  • Clarifying responsibility in discussions where multiple people are involved.

Common Misuses of Tagging:

  • Tagging in one-on-one chats: If you're already chatting directly with someone, tagging them is redundant.
  • Tagging the same person multiple times in one message: This can feel spammy and distracting.
  • Tagging everyone unnecessarily: Use @team or @channel only for critical announcements.
Remember: Tagging is a tool to improve clarity and communication — overusing it can have the opposite effect.
